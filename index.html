Research:

Security threats in Cognitive Radio Networks (CRNs)

Phd in Macquarie Universiy

Supervisor: Prof Vijay Varadharajan
Abstract: Cognitive radio (CR) is a promising technology for future wireless networks in order to efficiently utilize the limited spectrum resources and satisfy the rapidly increasing demand for wireless applications and services. However, CR wireless networks are susceptible to various security attacks. In my research, we identify such security threats and propose mitigation models to address such security threats.
